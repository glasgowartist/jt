John Taylor was born in Darvel, Ayrshire, Scotland in 1936. He gained a Diploma of Art in Drawing and Painting at Glasgow School of Art in 1959 and Higher Diploma of Art at Birmingham Polytechnic 1972-73 where he studied screenprinting.

Some essays on the artist's work by well-known writers:

  • The View from the Bunker, by Cordelia Oliver
  • New Work, 1991, by James Kelman
  • On a Thin Crust, 1996, by Donny O'Rourke
